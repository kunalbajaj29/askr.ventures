About Us

ASKR Ventures stands as a distinguished venture capital fund dedicated to strategic investments across diverse verticals within the blockchain industry. Recognizing the boundless potential for growth and innovation within this dynamic ecosystem, we actively seek
out visionary founders who possess the drive to shape the future. Our approach extends beyond financial investment, We thrive on providing Founders with the expertise and support they need to flourish on a global scale. Just as the blockchain landscape transcends geographical boundaries, we craft ingenious marketing strategies that transcend borders connecting products and ideas with
audiences worldwide. Our devotion to nurturing innovation is unwavering. Through strategic guidance, tailored mentorship, and
access to a robust network, we pave the way for our portfolio companies to reach new heights.
